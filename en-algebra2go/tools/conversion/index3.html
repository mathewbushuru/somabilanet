LENGTH
1 foot = 12 inches
1 yard = 3 feet
1 mile = 5,280 feet

1 inch = 2.54 cm

1 km = 1,000 m
1 m = 1,000 mm
1 m = 100 cm
1 m = 10 dm

VOLUME
1 cup = 8 oz
1 pint = 2 cups
1 quart = 2 pints
1 gal = 4 quarts

1 in3 = 16.39 mL
1 gal = 3.79 L
1 L = 1.06 quarts

1 mL = 1 cc (1 cm3)
1 L = 1,000 mL
1 L = 1,000,000 μL

WEIGHT/MASS
1 lb = 16 oz
1 T = 2,000 lb

1 oz = 28.3 g
1 kg = 2.20 lb

1 kg = 1,000 g

TEMPERATURE
C = (5/9)(F - 32)
F = (9/5)C + 32
